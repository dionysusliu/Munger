# Bob Stone

**Bob Stone** is a business strategy advisor specializing in organizational design and operational efficiency. He is best known for his advisory role at [[Atlas Dynamics]], where he focuses on aligning internal performance metrics with long-term customer success.

## Advisory Role
Bob serves as a strategic advisor to [[Atlas Dynamics]], a company founded by [[Alice Arbor]]. His work centers on restructuring how teams are motivated and how organizational learning is captured. By redesigning incentive frameworks and embedding continuous feedback mechanisms, Bob helps leadership maintain agility and drive sustainable growth.

## Key Focus Areas
- **Incentive Structures:** Developing compensation and recognition systems that reward long-term value creation, cross-functional collaboration, and customer-centric decision-making.
- **Feedback Loops:** Implementing rapid, data-driven feedback cycles that allow teams to quickly identify bottlenecks, iterate on processes, and improve service delivery.
- **Customer Retention Strategy:** Advising [[Atlas Dynamics]] on its adoption of the [[Flywheel model]], which replaces traditional linear sales funnels with a momentum-driven approach focused on customer satisfaction, repeat engagement, and organic retention.
