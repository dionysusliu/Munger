# Alice Arbor

**Alice Arbor** is a technology entrepreneur and business executive, widely recognized as the founder of [[Atlas Dynamics]]. She has been noted for her strategic leadership in [[systems engineering]], [[corporate innovation]], and [[technology commercialization]].

Arbor's professional background spans multiple disciplines within the [[technology sector]].
