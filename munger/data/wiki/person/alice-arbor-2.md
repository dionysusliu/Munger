# Alice Arbor

**Alice Arbor** is an entrepreneur and business executive best known as the founder of [[Atlas Dynamics]]. She is recognized for her strategic approach to sustainable business growth, particularly her implementation of cyclical growth frameworks and data-driven customer engagement models.

## Career and Founding
Arbor established [[Atlas Dynamics]] with a core mission to shift industry standards from transactional customer acquisition to long-term relationship building. Under her leadership, the company has developed scalable infrastructure designed to prioritize client success, operational efficiency, and continuous value delivery.

## Strategic Frameworks
A defining element of Arbor's executive strategy at [[Atlas Dynamics]] is the adoption of the [[Flywheel model]]. Moving away from traditional linear growth funnels, she implemented a customer-centric cycle focused on:
* **Momentum Generation:** Transforming positive customer experiences into organic advocacy and repeat engagement.
* **[[Customer retention]]:** Prioritizing post-onboarding support, personalized value delivery, and proactive churn reduction.
* **Continuous Optimization:** Using performance metrics to iteratively refine service delivery and product roadmaps.

## Advisory and Collaboration
To align internal operations with external growth objectives, Arbor partners closely with [[Bob Stone]], a specialist in organizational design and behavioral strategy. Stone provides strategic counsel to [[Atlas Dynamics]] in the following areas:
* Developing performance-aligned [[Incentive structures]] for both internal teams and external partners.
* Engineering closed-loop [[Feedback loops]] to accelerate product iteration and customer success initiatives.
* Integrating behavioral economics principles into retention and engagement workflows.
