# Bob Stone

**Bob Stone** is a business strategist and organizational consultant specializing in behavioral incentive design and system feedback architecture. He currently serves as a strategic advisor to [[Atlas Dynamics]], where he works alongside founder [[Alice Arbor]] to optimize long-term growth and operational alignment.

## Advisory Role
Stone provides targeted consulting to [[Atlas Dynamics]], focusing on the development and integration of:
- **Incentive Structures**: Designing performance-based frameworks that align employee, partner, and stakeholder behaviors with organizational goals.
- **Feedback Loops**: Implementing iterative data-capture systems that enable rapid course correction and continuous process improvement.

His advisory contributions directly reinforce [[Atlas Dynamics]]' adoption of the [[Flywheel Model]], a momentum-driven operational framework the company uses to systematically improve [[Customer Retention]] and reduce acquisition costs over time.

## Core Expertise

- Closed-loop system design and analytics
- Customer lifecycle optimization
- Scaling organizational performance metrics
