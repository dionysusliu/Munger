# Alice Arbor

**Alice Arbor** is an entrepreneur and business executive best known as the founder and CEO of [[Atlas Dynamics]], a company distinguished by its application of the [[flywheel model]] to enhance customer retention and sustainable business growth.

## Career

### Atlas Dynamics
Arbor established [[Atlas Dynamics]] with a mission to transform traditional approaches to customer relationship management. Under her leadership, the company has become recognized for implementing systems that prioritize long-term customer value over transactional interactions.

The company's core methodology centers on the [[flywheel model]], a strategic framework that treats business momentum as a self-reinforcing cycle where customer satisfaction generates referrals, which in turn drive new acquisition and further engagement.

## Business Philosophy

### Customer Retention Strategy
Arbor's approach at [[Atlas Dynamics]] emphasizes:
- **Compounding Growth**: Building systems where each satisfied customer contributes to organizational momentum
- **Friction Reduction**: Identifying and eliminating barriers that impede the customer journey
- **Sustainable Engagement**: Creating feedback mechanisms that continuously improve the user experience

## Advisory Network

To support [[Atlas Dynamics]]' organizational development, Arbor has engaged strategic advisors specializing in behavioral economics and system design. [[Bob Stone]] serves as a principal advisor to the company, providing expertise on:
- Incentive structure architecture
- Feedback loop optimization
- Performance-driven organizational design

Stone's advisory role focuses on aligning internal incentive systems with the company's flywheel-driven customer retention goals.
