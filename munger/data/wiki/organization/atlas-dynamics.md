 # Atlas Dynamics

**Atlas Dynamics** is a company founded by [[Alice Arbor]] that utilizes the [[flywheel model]] as its core strategic framework to drive sustainable growth and enhance [[customer retention]]. The company is recognized for its systematic approach to building momentum through positive customer experiences and self-reinforcing business cycles.

## History

Atlas Dynamics was established by [[Alice Arbor]], who developed the company's foundational philosophy around sustainable growth mechanics and customer-centric operations. Under Arbor's leadership, the organization has implemented innovative retention strategies that distinguish it from traditional sales-driven business models.

## Business Model

The company operates on the [[flywheel model]], a business methodology that prioritizes delivering exceptional customer value to create a self-sustaining growth cycle. Key components of Atlas Dynamics' approach include:

*   **Customer Retention Focus**: Prioritizing long-term relationships over one-time transactions
*   **Momentum Building**: Using satisfied customers as a driving force for referrals and repeat business
*   **Friction Reduction**: Continuously optimizing processes to remove barriers to customer success
*   **Compounding Growth**: Leveraging existing customer satisfaction to attract new business organically

## Leadership and Advisory

### Alice Arbor
[[Alice Arbor]] is the founder of Atlas Dynamics, responsible for establishing the company's strategic direction and implementing the flywheel methodology as the central organizational framework.

### Bob Stone
[[Bob Stone]] serves as a strategic advisor to Atlas Dynamics, specializing in:
*   Designing effective [[incentive structures]] to align team behavior with company goals
*   Implementing [[feedback loops]] to accelerate learning and optimization
*   Advising on mechanisms that maintain and increase the momentum of the company's flywheel
